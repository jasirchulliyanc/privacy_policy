Account Deletion Instructions

Follow these steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the Doctor Finder on your device.
  2. Navigate to your profile page.
  3. Click on the "Delete Account" button.
  4. A confirmation dialog will appear. Click on "Delete" to confirm.
  5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our support team at [your support email].

Note: Deleting your account is irreversible and will result in the loss of all your data.
